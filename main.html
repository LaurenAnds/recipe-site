Course 3 - Firecracker Beef

Type: Main Course
Serves: 4 People
Prep time: 3 minutes
Cook time: 10 minutes
Vegan/Vegetarian: No


Ingredients


For the beef:


  • 1 1/2 tbsp oil
  • 500g beef mince
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp chili flakes
  • 3 tbsp water

For the sauce:


  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp rice vinegar
  • 4 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 4 tbsp brown sugar

Method

  1. Mix the sauce ingredients in a bowl and set aside.

  2. Heat oil in a large frypan over high heat. Add beef and cook, breaking it up as you go, until you can no longer see raw meat (2Â½ minutes). Add garlic and chilli flakes and cook for 1 minute.

  3. Add Firecracker sauce ingredients, stir to coat the beef. Cook for 5 to 7 minutes, stirring only every minute or so initially, then more towards the end, until the sauce reduces and you can see the beef caramelising. Caramelise well for good flavour!

  4. Add the water and cook for 1 minute (the water makes the beef saucier!)

  5. Serve over rice, sprinkled with sesame seeds, green onion and a squirt of extra sriracha if you're feeling brave! Add a pile of vegetables of choice on the side. Dive in!
