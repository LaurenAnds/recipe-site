Hello! We created THe Great Newport Menu as a twist on The Great British Menu tv series. We decided to select a starter, fish course, main and dessert so that you can prepare an entire night of a dinner party without having to think of anything, just add wine!

Each recipe has been hand picked by our experts for ease of cooking and the ability to make some dishes in advance of your party. The recipes have been tried and tested and approved by our home cooks so the difficulty should be relatively easy. Contact us to let us know how you got on.
